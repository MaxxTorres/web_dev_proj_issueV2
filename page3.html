Proper Disposal

More and more e-waste recycling bins are being implemented. Remember to use these when necessary because this allows for proper processing of e-waste.

Tech Reusal

Instead of throwing away old or used electronics, try repairing or reselling these items. Even better, try to buy new gadgets only when needed.

Greener Production

Tech manufacturers also play a big role in mitigating e-waste. They need to design electronics that use more sustainable and durable materials which harm the environment the least.

Law Enforcement

Another group involved are the national and international sectors. They must implement laws and regulations which properly control e-waste.
